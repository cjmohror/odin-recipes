Colton's World Famous Pizza Recipe

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a classic favorite known for its crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and savory slices of pepperoni.
The combination of flavors creates a perfect balance of tangy, cheesy, and slightly spicy notes that make it a popular choice for pizza lovers of all ages.

This recipe starts with a homemade or store-bought pizza dough that is stretched and topped with a layer of pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni slices.
After baking until golden brown and bubbly, the pizza is finished with optional seasonings such as oregano, parmesan cheese, or red pepper flakes for extra flavor.
Served hot from the oven, it makes a delicious meal for family dinners, gatherings, or game nights.

Here are the ingredients you will need:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese- Mozzarella
  • Pepperonis
  • Seasonings: oregano, chili flakes, parmesan

Steps to make your delicious pizza:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and prepare a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  2. Roll or stretch the pizza dough into a round shape and place it on the prepared pan.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and arrange pepperoni slices evenly on top.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Voila, there you have it. Enjoy your delicious food!
